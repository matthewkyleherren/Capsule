about

Kryptoz began as a proof-of-concept for PETITE FLEUR to demonstrate our knowledge of the web3 space.

After multiple sell-out collections and high-profile collaborations, The Kryptoz has grown into a fully-fledged community ready to head into the metaverse! Kryptoz holders are given access to The Kryptoz-verse, an art-forward, community-driven and collaborative space.

The Kryptoz IP has been commissioned by AMC Theatres, Grammy award-winning producers, tech startups, luxury brands and more. If you’re interested in learning more about commission opportunities - visit our contact page
