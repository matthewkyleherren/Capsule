How everything started


With over 15 years of experience in creating exceptional work for VFX, games, and virtual worlds, Jon Shelley is a true industry veteran. It was his vision that led to the creation of PETITE FLEUR, a creative studio that strives to be the best in the metaverse.

our team of highly skilled artists and creatives work closely with clients to understand their unique needs and deliver exceptional results.

Whether you're looking to bring a new product to market, create an immersive web3 experience, or design a cutting-edge metaverse project, we have the expertise and passion to make it happen.

So if you're looking for a partner that can bring your ideas to life in a truly spectacular way, look no further than PETITE FLEUR.

At VRTL WLRD, we live and breathe metaverse and web3! We are a team of tech enthusiasts who are dedicated to creating exceptional digital media content that not only satisfies our clients but also fulfils our own creative aspirations. We are fuelled by our passion and believe that every detail matters in shaping the future of the digital world.

➀

TECHNOLOGY ORIENTED MINDSET

The entire PETITE FLEUR team has a shared interest and love for all things technology, which is why we always try out new pieces of tech and programs to upgrade our creative process.

➁

Beauty is in the details

Focusing on the details is like a type of meditation to us. In our years working in this field, we have learned that the 3D projects that stand out the most are the ones with the most attention to details.

➂

art our core

At our core, we believe that true mastery of the digital realm requires hands-on experience in its creation. That's why our team boasts years of production know-how across diverse disciplines. We're not just a middleman agency; we're the creative minds behind the art itself.
